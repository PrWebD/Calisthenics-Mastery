Front Lever Pull

Expert

The front lever pull is an extremely advanced dynamic skill that combines the front lever position with a pull-up motion. Starting from a front lever, the practitioner pulls themselves upward while maintaining the horizontal body position. This movement requires not only the strength for a static front lever but also explosive pulling power. It represents one of the highest levels of relative strength in calisthenics and serves as a bridge to even more advanced skills.

Muscles Worked:

Primary: Latissimus Dorsi, Biceps, Rhomboids, Teres Major

Secondary: Core (for maintaining position), Forearms, Rear Deltoids

Stabilizers: Trapezius, Infraspinatus, Wrist Flexors, Hip Flexors
