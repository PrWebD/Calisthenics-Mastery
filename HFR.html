Human Flag Raise

Expert

The human flag raise is a dynamic progression of the static human flag, where the practitioner lifts their body from a hanging position into a full human flag. This movement builds the specific strength needed for the static hold, particularly in the obliques and lateral core muscles. Proper form involves initiating the movement with the lower body while maintaining tension in the upper body, creating a coordinated "unfurling" motion. This exercise develops the explosive core strength required for the full skill.

Muscles Worked:

Primary: Obliques, Quadratus Lumborum, Latissimus Dorsi, Core

Secondary: Grip muscles, Hip Abductors/Adductors, Shoulder stabilizers

Stabilizers: Transverse Abdominis, Spinal Erectors, Glutes, Quadriceps
