Planche Press

Expert

The planche press is a dynamic skill that begins in a planche position and transitions into a front lever or similar hanging position by shifting the body's center of gravity. This movement requires not only the strength for a planche but also the control to smoothly transition between extreme positions. The press involves coordinated movement of the shoulders, core, and limbs to transfer from a pushing to a pulling orientation while maintaining body tension.

Muscles Worked:

Primary: Anterior Deltoids, Pectorals, Latissimus Dorsi, Core

Secondary: Serratus Anterior, Biceps, Triceps, Forearms

Stabilizers: Rotator Cuff, Transverse Abdominis, Hip Flexors, Spinal Erectors
